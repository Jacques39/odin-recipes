Shrimp Salsa

This is an awesomely simple, light, and refreshing salsa that goes great with tortilla chips.

Shrimp Salsa Recipe

Ingredients

  • ½ pound salad shrimp
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • ½ red onion, diced
  • ¼ cup minced cilantro
  • ¼ cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • clove garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Stir the salad shrimp, tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, pepper, and garlic together in a large glass bowl.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap.
  3. Refrigerate until the flavors combine, at least 1 hour.
  4. Serve cold.
